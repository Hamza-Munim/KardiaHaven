DIRECTIONS

CLICK HERE FOR MOBILE MAP

From North Bay:

  • Take HW-305 South towards Parkview
  • Go across the Green River Bridge
  • Take the HW-305 South ramp towards CITY CENTER/WILLOW ST.
  • Stay on WILLOW ST. until MAPLE AVE
  • Turn RIGHT on MAPLE AVE.
  • Turn LEFT on ELM ST.
  • Take ELM ST. through the ELM TUNNEL.
  • Proceed through the major intersection of BIRCH and ELM.
  • Turn RIGHT on OAK ST.
  • Turn LEFT on PINE ST.
  • DESTINATION is at the corner of PINE ST. & ASH ST.

From East Bay:

  • Go Westbound on the BAYWAY BRIDGE (I-90)
  • Take the OAKWOOD ST. exit.
  • At the bottom of the exit ramp turn LEFT on OAKWOOD ST.
  • Go ACROSS Cedar St.
  • Turn LEFT on SPRUCE ST.
  • Turn RIGHT on PINEWOOD ST.
  • Turn RIGHT on BIRCH
  • DESTINATION is at the corner of BIRCH ST. & OAK ST.

From South Bay:

  • Take 380 NORTH to GREENVILLE
  • GET in LEFT LANE as you see the Greenville cityscape
  • LEFT off the ramp onto FOREST DRIVE
  • Continue straight past CENTRAL PARK
  • You are now on RIVERSIDE
  • Pass under the Pine Bridge and past the CENTRAL PLAZA BUILDING.
  • Turn LEFT on MAIN at stop light.
  • Turn LEFT on MAPLE ST.
  • Turn LEFT on ELM ST.
  • DESTINATION is at the corner of ELM ST. & OAK ST.
